Our Mission

Our dream is to increase the level of safety and security for all those who love the city of Tel Aviv.

We seek your partnership in reporting missing items due to theft or loss.

We offer a platform to track lost / found items and to raise awareness around petty crime.

Many of us have experienced a break-in, theft of a bike, wallet or phone.

By posting these events, it warns others of the criminal activity and creates a reliable set of metrics which may be used to push for further social change.
